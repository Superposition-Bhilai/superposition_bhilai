About SuperPosition

Superposition is an international non-profit bridging the gender gap in Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics (STEM). Our mission is to empower women in STEM by creating educational opportunities and supportive environments in which they can develop their passions. The Headquarters of the Superposition organization is situated in San Francisco, California, USA. In April 2022 we received the approval for Superposition Bhilai Chapter and initiated it at SSTC Bhilai in the CSE department.
